Crystal Collector

Instructions

You will be assigned a random number at the beginning of the game. Below are the four crystals. Clicking on a crystal will add a certain numberical value to your total score. In order to win the game, your total score must match or come as close as possible to the random integer assigned. If your score goes over this random assignmed value, you lose. You cannot see the value of each crystal until you click on it. Everytime the game restarts, the crystals are assigned a new number.
